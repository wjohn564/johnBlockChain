Problem Definition

A modern day traditional course enrollment system faces many challenges such as lack of transparency, data tampering and are overdependent on centralised servers that are prone to cyberattacks. These limitations undermine efficiency, and more importantly in managing academic records.

Why use a Blockchain?

A Blockchain provides a decentralised, secure, and transparent solution to the above challenges. It can accomplish this be leveraging smart contracts, and immutable transactions.

My System Leverages:

  • Tamper-proof Records: Course data and enrollments are securely recorded on the blockchain, preventing unauthorised modifications.
  • Decentralised Integrity: The blockchain ensures data is distributed and less reliant on centralised systems, improving reliability.
  • Efficient Processes: Blockchain-based smart contracts automate core actions like creating courses and managing enrollments, reducing administrative overhead.

Our system enhances the integrity and efficiency of course management, providing a secure and reliable platform for students and institutions.
